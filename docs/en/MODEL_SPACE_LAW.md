# National Space Law Proposal (Draft Articles)

## Article 1 - Purpose
To protect Turkey's national rights in space and ensure peaceful activities in accordance with international law.

## Article 3 - National Space Registry
All space objects launched by Turkish entities must be registered within the National Space Registry managed by TUA.

## Article 5 - Orbital Sovereignty
Turkish orbital slots and frequency spectrums are classified as "National Strategic Assets". Any unauthorized interference will be countered based on reciprocity and international law.
