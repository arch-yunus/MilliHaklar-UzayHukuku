# Strategic Framework: National Space Rights & Law

## Overview
As Turkey advances its National Space Program, the protection of orbital assets and frequency rights has become a matter of national sovereignty. This "Legal-Tech" ecosystem provides a robust architecture to defend these interests against cyber and physical threats.

## Core Pillars
1. **Dynamic SSA Implementation:** Real-time tracking of space assets using orbital mechanics and signal processing.
2. **Automated Legal Attribution:** Instant mapping of technical infringements to international treaties (OST 1967, Liability Convention).
3. **Sovereignty Shield:** Proactive legislative proposals for the first National Space Law of Turkey.
