# Executive Abstract: National Space Rights Defense

## Project Vision
As space becomes the new "high ground" for national sovereignty, this project proposes a **Three-Layered Legal-Tech Defense Shield** to protect Turkey's strategic space assets. By integrating real-time Space Situational Awareness (SSA) data with international space law frameworks, we bridge the gap between technical monitoring and legal enforcement.

## The Three-Layered Model
1.  **Technical Detection (SSA):** Continuous monitoring of orbit infringements, frequency interference, and debris threats using TLE and RF analysis.
2.  **Legal Attribution:** Real-time mapping of technical anomalies to specific articles in international treaties (e.g., Article 51 of the UN Charter, 1972 Liability Convention).
3.  **Diplomatic Response:** Automated generation of evidence packages for institutional use (TUA, Ministry of Foreign Affairs) to safeguard national interests in the UN and ITU.

## Strategic Impact
This ecosystem ensures Turkey's "use it or lose it" orbital slots are protected and provides a robust framework for the upcoming **National Space Law**.
