# Contributing to Milli Haklar - Uzay Hukuku

Thank you for your interest in contributing to our research on National Space Rights and Space Law.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Reporting Issues**: If you find inaccuracies in legal citations or technical data, please open an issue.
2.  **Pull Requests**: 
    -   Ensure your content is academically rigorous.
    -   Follow the existing markdown structure.
    -   Cite international treaties (Outer Space Treaty, etc.) correctly.
3.  **Academic Integrity**: Always provide sources for legal theories or technical proposals.
